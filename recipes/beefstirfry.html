Quick Beef Stir-fry

Description

This beef stir-fry is the perfect recipe to fill you up. The quick recipe makes for an easy quick, yet delicious meal.

ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 pound of been sirloin,cut into 2-inch strips
  • 1 1/2 cups of fresh broccoli
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into matchsticks
  • 2 carrots, thinly sliced
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 teasponn minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds, toasted

Steps

After you have gathered all ingredients:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat; cook and sitr beef until browned 3-4 minutes.
  2. Set beef aside, add broccoli, bell pepper, carrots, green onion, and garlic to the wok/skillet. Cook and stir for 2 minutes
  3. Stir beef into vegetables and season with soy sauce and sesame seeds. Continue to cook and stir until vegetables are tender, about 2 more minutes.
  4. Serve your food! Feel free to add any other sides!
