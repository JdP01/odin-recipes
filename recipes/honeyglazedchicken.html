Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

The Honey glazed chicken is a simple, delicious recipe that should only take you about 20 minutes to prepare. This recipe Will provide you with about 2-4 servings. With a delicious taste and a simple recipe this is a great and healthy meal for you.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1/8 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into small pieces

Steps

After you have gathered all of your ingredients:

  1. Mix honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook adn stir chicken in hot oil until lightly boerwn, about 5 minutes.
  3. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more
  4. Serve your food! feel free to add any other sides!
